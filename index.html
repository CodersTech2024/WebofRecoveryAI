About the Project

RecoveryAI is an innovative solution that uses artificial intelligence to help prevent drug addiction, assist in recovery, and provide early warning signs of relapse. This system analyzes behavior patterns, tracks health data, and provides personalized support for people recovering from substance use disorders.

Goals

  • Use AI to detect relapse signs early.
  • Provide support tools for addicts and their families.
  • Educate the public on drug misuse through interactive data and AI insights.

Global Drug Abuse Statistics

How RecoveryAI Works

The system uses machine learning to analyze data from users, including mood logs, chat responses, and physical symptoms (from connected health trackers if available). It alerts caretakers or professionals when a risk is detected.

Key Features:

  • Chatbot assistant for daily check-ins
  • Emotion detection from responses
  • Recovery progress tracker
  • Educational AI quizzes for awareness
