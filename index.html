RecoveryAI

Using Artificial Intelligence to Fight Drug Addiction & Save Lives

🧠 What is RecoveryAI?

RecoveryAI is an intelligent solution designed to assist in detecting, preventing, and treating drug addiction using Artificial Intelligence. It analyzes behavior patterns, detects early signs of relapse, and connects people with the help they need — when they need it most.

🚨 The Problem

Millions of people suffer from drug addiction worldwide, with limited access to real-time support. Traditional systems often miss early warning signs. Relapse rates are high, and many people feel alone in their recovery.

🎯 Our Goals

✅ Detect relapse signs using AI analysis
✅ Support recovery with smart daily check-ins
✅ Raise awareness through AI-powered education
✅ Help doctors and families with smart alerts

🧩 Features of RecoveryAI

💬 Chatbot check-ins with emotion detection
📈 Mood & habit tracking dashboard
🔔 Alert system for high-risk users
📚 Interactive quizzes to educate and engage

🤖 How AI Helps

AI algorithms in RecoveryAI learn from user behavior, recognize emotional changes, and trigger alerts when a user may be at risk of relapse. It also adapts its support messages and educational content based on each user's journey.
